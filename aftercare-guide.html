INVESTMENT PRESERVATION

High-end body jewelry from Ethereal Ink is designed to last a lifetime, but it requires specific care to maintain its biocompatibility and luster.

Sterile Cleaning

For gold and titanium pieces, use only pH-balanced, fragrance-free soaps. Avoid ultrasonic cleaners for organic stones like Opals, as the vibrations can fracture the gemstone structure.

  • No Alcohol
  • No Peroxide
  • Saline Soak Ready

Mechanical Checks

"Check your ends." Threaded and threadless jewelry can gradually loosen with movement. We recommend checking the tightness of your jewelry ends once a week with clean hands.

  • Righty-Tighty
  • Wash Hands First

Safe Storage

When not in use, store your pieces in individual, soft-lined compartments. Gold is a soft metal and can easily be scratched by Titanium or harder gemstones if stored together.

  • Scratch Protection
  • Dry Environment
