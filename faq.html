Technical insights into our clinical procedures, material standards, and safety protocols. Knowledge is the first step to a successful modification.

01. Clinical Procedures

We utilize a multi-stage sterilization process centered around the Statim G4 vacuum autoclave. This hospital-grade device uses pressurized steam to eliminate 99.9% of biological contaminants.

Every tool, piece of jewelry, and needle is processed in an individual indicator pouch that changes color once full sterilization has been achieved. We maintain digital logs of every cycle for quality assurance.

Piercing guns utilize blunt force trauma to push a dull stud through your tissue, which can lead to significant scarring and complications. Furthermore, guns are made of plastic and cannot be sterilized in an autoclave, posing a severe cross-contamination risk. We exclusively use single-use, hollow surgical needles for a clean, precision modification.

02. Material Integrity

We strictly adhere to APP (Association of Professional Piercers) standards. For initial procedures, we only utilize:

  • **ASTM-F136 Implant Grade Titanium:** Hypoallergenic and nickel-free.
  • **14k/18k Solid Gold:** Nickel-free and alloyed for biocompatibility.
  • **Niobium:** A 99.9% pure elemental metal.

To ensure a safe and successful healing process, we can only pierce with jewelry purchased from Ethereal Ink. This allows us to verify the metal's mill certificate and guarantee its surface polish quality. We can, however, install your personal heirloom jewelry into HEALED piercings after it has been clinically cleaned by our staff.

03. Logistics & Support

Standard procedures require you to be 18+ with a valid government photo ID. Minors (13-17) may be pierced for select procedures with a parent or legal guardian present, biological birth certificate, and matching photo IDs for both parties.

Please note: We do not perform any modifications on infants or children under the age of 13 for any reason.

Rescheduling can be done through your digital portal or by emailing the studio at least 48 hours prior to your session. Deposits are non-refundable but can be transferred to a new date once with proper notice. No-shows or late cancellations will forfeit their session fee.
