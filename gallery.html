Case Study 01

THE GOLDEN RATIO

This project involved a multi-phase ear curation for a client seeking a "celestial" theme. Over a period of 14 months, our senior artist Jax Sterling mapped the client's unique ear anatomy to place seven distinct pieces of 14k solid gold and genuine moonstone.

The challenge was balancing the horizontal space of the conch with the vertical rise of the helix. By utilizing threadless jewelry from Buddha Jewelry Organics, we were able to create a secure, low-profile aesthetic that allows for daily movement without irritation.

THE MACRO PERSPECTIVE

We believe that documentation is an essential part of the professional piercing process. High-resolution macro photography allows us to inspect our work at magnifications impossible to the naked eye, ensuring that every angle is correct and every piece of jewelry is seated perfectly.

Our studio utilizes specialized lighting and mirrorless camera systems to capture the true color of the metals and the clarity of the gemstones. We prioritize client consent and privacy; every image in this archive was captured with full written authorization and a shared passion for the craft.
