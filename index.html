A LEGACY OF
PRECISION & ARTISTRY

Founded in 2018, Ethereal Ink was born from a singular vision: to elevate the body piercing experience beyond the backrooms of traditional tattoo shops. We believed that body modification deserved a dedicated space that prioritized medical-grade hygiene as much as artistic vision.

Our journey began in a small one-room studio with a commitment to never using piercing guns and only stocking the world's finest jewelry. Over the years, we have grown into a multi-disciplinary collective of artists, each specializing in unique aspects of body adornment, from complex ear curations to subtle facial aesthetics.

At Ethereal Ink, we don't just perform a service; we facilitate a transformation. Every client who walks through our doors is treated with the utmost respect and care. We understand that for many, a new piercing is a milestone, a form of reclamation, or a celebration of self-identity. It is our honor to be a part of those stories.

THE ETHEREAL STANDARD

We set the bar for safety and quality in the body art industry. Here is why clients trust us with their most personal adornments.

Aseptic Piercing

Every procedure is performed using a strict non-touch technique. All skin preparation uses medical-grade antiseptic solutions to ensure your safety and minimize infection risks.

Biocompatible Metals

We only use ASTM-F136 compliant Titanium and nickel-free 14k/18k Solid Gold. Our jewelry is hand-polished to a mirror finish to eliminate microscopic burrs that can cause irritation.

Aftercare Education

Our relationship doesn't end when the needle is withdrawn. We provide detailed, science-based aftercare instructions and lifetime troubleshooting support for all our clients.

Expert Curation

Our piercers are also stylists. We specialize in "Curated Ear" projects, designing unique jewelry placements that harmonize with your natural anatomy and personal aesthetic.
