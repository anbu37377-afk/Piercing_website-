MATERIAL INTEGRITY

At Ethereal Ink, we believe that what you put inside your body is just as important as how it looks on the outside. This is why we exclusively stock internal-thread or threadless jewelry from the world's leading manufacturers.

Our **Implant Grade Titanium (ASTM-F136)** is sourced from verified medical suppliers. Unlike surgical steel—which often contains hidden nickel and lead—our titanium is hypoallergenic, corrosion-resistant, and lighter than steel. It's the same material used in heart valves and tooth implants for a reason: the body loves it.

When it comes to **Solid gold**, we never compromise. Our 14k and 18k gold collections are free of toxic alloys like cadmium. Each piece is hand-polished to a "mirror finish." This isn't just for shine; a smoother surface means fewer microscopic places for bacteria to grow, leading to a faster and healthier healing process.

GEMSTONE ETHICS

Every sparkle has a story. Our collection features hand-set gemstones, from conflict-free diamonds to ethically sourced semi-precious stones like genuine Opal, Moonstone, and Turquoise.

We utilize **Swarovski Zirconia** for our budget-conscious collections, ensuring that even our more accessible pieces feature the finest precision-cut crystals in the industry. For our high-end luxury requests, we work with guest lapidaries to source rare stones that are custom-cut for the body.

Unlike many "fast-fashion" jewelry brands, our stones are held in place by secure prongs or bezel settings—never glue. This ensures the longevity of your investment and prevents stones from falling out during daily wear or cleaning cycles.

INVESTMENT PROTECTION GUIDE

Sizing for Success

Body jewelry sizing involves three main measurements: **Gauge** (the thickness of the post), **Length** (for studs/barbells), and **Diameter** (for hoops). Using a piece that is too short can leads to "embedding," while too long can cause "migration."

  • 18g/16g: Standard for most ear & facial piercings.
  • 14g: Common for navels, nipples, and industrials.
  • 20g: Classic lobe size (not recommended for cartilage).

Daily Maintenance

Fine jewelry requires fine care. For healed piercings, we recommend removing jewelry every 30 days for deep cleaning in an ultrasonic bath or with a soft-bristled brush and mild soap.

*Never use harsh chemicals like hydrogen peroxide or alcohol on your fine jewelry, especially pieces containing organic stones like Opal or Turquoise.
